Welcome to the [Lens_Flare](https://docs.unity3d.com/Packages/com.unity.render-pipelines.high-definition@14.0/manual/shared/lens-flare/lens-flare-component.html) Samples.

This sample contains 25 Lens Flare presets that are available in LensFlareSamples/LensFlarePresets. They can be assigned to the *Lens *Flare *Data property of a *Lens *Flare *(SRP) component. 
This scene contains a script that will assign the presets onto the point light to easily browse through them.

Enter play mode to see how they look !
