
simple, rough system of route

Usage:

A. ROUTE
  1. create new GameObject;
  2. add 'CCRoute' script on it;
  3. make children GameObjects = route points.
B. VEHICLE
  1. add your vehicle to scene ( + rigidbody, collider);
  2. add 'CCVehicle' script on it;
  3. add your route (see A.1.) to 'Route' slot;
  4. adjust intuitive params;
  5. DONE.
